About

Dr. Tulsi Pawan Fowdur received his BEng (Hons) degree in Electronic and Communication Engineering with First Class Honours from the University of Mauritius. He was awarded a Gold Medal for the best undergraduate engineering project and subsequently obtained a full-time PhD scholarship before completing his doctorate in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

He is presently an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Mauritius. His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Mobile Communications, Networking, Cybersecurity, Telecommunications Applications Development, Internet of Things, Industry 4.0, and Energy Efficient Communications.

He has authored over 150 scientific publications, including nine books, and actively contributes to the international research community through keynote speeches, editorial activities, conference organisation, research supervision, and international collaborations.
